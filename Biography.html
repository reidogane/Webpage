▶︎Profile

Rei Dogane & The Sidemen is a 4-piece rock 'n' roll band. In 2019, the band was formed in Kobe City, Hyogo Prefecture, Japan.
The band consists of Rei Dogane (Vo. & Gt.), Haru (Gt.), Jiyu (Ba.), and Shunya (Dr.).
